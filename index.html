How It Works

Five steps.
Thousands of
plans generated.

01
Gym inputs their equipment inventory
The gym owner or manager logs every machine, cable station, free weight, and cardio piece they have in GymIQ's web dashboard. This takes 20 minutes and becomes the foundation of every plan. GymIQ only ever recommends equipment that physically exists at that location.
02
New member completes a 2-minute intake
When a member joins, they answer a short goal questionnaire — fitness level, goals (muscle, fat loss, endurance), days available per week, any injuries or limitations. Frictionless. Mobile-first. Done before they leave the front desk.
03
AI generates a personalised, location-specific plan
GymIQ instantly creates a structured week-by-week workout program using only that gym's equipment. The plan matches the member's specific goals and adapts progressively as they track their sessions. No generic templates. No one-size-fits-all.
04
Certified trainers review and validate
AI-generated plans go through a trainer review layer for safety and effectiveness. This human validation is GymIQ's moat — it's not just an algorithm, it's trainer-backed programming. This makes the product credible to gym chains and members alike.
05
Delivered via the gym's white-labelled web app
Members access their plan through a web app branded with the gym's name and logo — no download required. Works on any device, instantly. The gym looks premium and tech-forward without building anything. GymIQ is the engine under the hood — invisible, seamless, powerful.
